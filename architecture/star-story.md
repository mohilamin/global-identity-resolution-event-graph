# STAR Story

## Situation

Large consumer platforms receive fragmented events from devices, cookies, sessions, ads, purchases, support, and account recovery flows.

## Task

Build a local platform that resolves identity, builds attribution paths, and detects suspicious identity clusters.

## Action

Implemented deterministic matching, probabilistic scoring, graph exports, session stitching, conversion attribution, fraud cluster detection, scorecards, API, dashboard, tests, Docker, and CI.

## Result

Produced a FAANG-relevant portfolio project showing event-scale identity resolution, graph analytics, attribution, and trust infrastructure.
